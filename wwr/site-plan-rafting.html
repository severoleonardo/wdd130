Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating a website about the White Water Rafting company is to provide information about the services offered by the company and attract new customers interested in whitewater adventures. The goal is to present a clear view of the travel options, including packages and prices, so that site visitors can easily plan and book their adventures. In addition, the website can be used to increase the visibility of the company by highlighting its qualities and experiences, as well as providing testimonials and reviews from satisfied customers. The website can also be used to showcase photos and videos of the trips to give a clearer idea of the experience offered by the company. When creating a website about the White Water Rafting company, it is important to keep in mind that the goal is to provide a pleasant and informative experience to visitors, encouraging them to book and share their experiences with others.

Audience

The target audience for this website about the White Water Rafting company are adventure seekers who are looking for a thrilling and memorable outdoor experience. These customers are typically in the age range of 18-45 and have a moderate to high level of income, allowing them to afford recreational activities like whitewater rafting. They are interested in outdoor activities, adventure sports, and exploring nature. They want a unique and exciting experience that provides a sense of adventure and allows them to connect with nature. They are seeking an escape from their daily routines and are looking to create unforgettable memories with friends or family. Their needs include access to detailed information about the packages and services offered by the White Water Rafting company, as well as clear instructions on how to book and prepare for the trip. They also value safety and want to feel assured that the company takes all necessary precautions to ensure their well-being during the trip. The audience will most likely access the website through their desktop or mobile devices, as they may be researching and booking their trip while on the go or during their leisure time.
